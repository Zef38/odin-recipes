Homemade Pizza

Instructions

1

Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (240–260°C).

2

Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

3

Spread tomato sauce evenly, leaving a small border for the crust.

4

Add mozzarella cheese and your preferred toppings.

5

Bake for 8–12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

6

Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
